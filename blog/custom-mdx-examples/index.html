Math Expressions

Astrofolio allows you to render mathematical expressions in MDX posts using KaTeX . Simply wrap your expression in $ to include KaTeX within your MDX content.

$
\int_0^\infty e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$

Renders:
